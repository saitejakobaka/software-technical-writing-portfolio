# Enable Automatic Chat Backup in WhatsApp

## Purpose

This procedure describes how to enable automatic chat backup in the WhatsApp application.

## Prerequisites

* WhatsApp must be installed and logged in.
* Internet connection should be active.

## Steps

1. Open **WhatsApp** on your phone.
2. Tap the **three dots** in the top-right corner.
3. Select **Settings** from the menu.
4. Tap **Chats** > **Chat Backup**.
5. Tap **Back up to Google Drive**.
6. Choose how often you want WhatsApp to back up chats (Daily, Weekly, Monthly).
7. Select your Google account and tap **Back Up** to confirm.

> **Tip:** Ensure your phone is connected to Wi-Fi before performing the backup to avoid data charges.



## Notes

* You can disable automatic backups anytime by selecting **Never** in Step 6.

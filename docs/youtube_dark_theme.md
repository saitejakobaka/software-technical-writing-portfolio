# Enable Dark theme in YouTube Application

## Purpose
This procedure describes how to enable the dark theme in the youtube mobile app.
The dark theme reduces eye strain and improves the visibility in low-light environment.

## Prerequisites
- Internet connection must be active.
- YouTube app installed and logged in.

## Steps
1. Open **YouTube** on your phone.
2. Tap your **Profile icon** > **Settings** > **General** > **Appearance**. 

    ![YouTube Appearance setting showing Dark theme option](images/enable_dark_theme.jpg)


3. Select **Dark theme**.
4. After you complete these steps, the YouTube interface switches to the dark mode.

> **Tip:** Select **Use device theme** to match your phone's system setting.
> **Note:** To revert, choose **Light theme** in Step 3.
